Master's Desk

Welcome to Master's Desk!

Our mission is to help students embark on transformative journeys by pursuing their master’s degrees abroad, enriching their lives through intercultural exchange. We offer personalized support and resources, from pre-departure workshops to cultural orientation, ensuring students feel confident and prepared for their international studies. We believe in empowering each student to discover their unique identity as a global learner and scholar, and we provide tailored academic coaching, accommodations for students with disabilities, and language support for English language learners. By fostering strong connections with faculty and staff, we enhance our study abroad initiatives and ensure that every student has the tools and guidance they need to thrive in diverse environments.

How to get started?

  • Send us a request of interest by filling out a form : https://forms.gle/3X4WtzXn687kmZVf7
  • Counseling Services from DSU : Expert guidance to help you navigate the application process.
  • We connect to the University : Direct link to the university’s Master's Desk counselors for personalized advice.
  • Application Screening : Thorough review of your application to ensure completeness and competitiveness.
  • Direct University Support : Ongoing assistance from the university throughout the Master's Desk process.
