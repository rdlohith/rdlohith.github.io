Admissions

Students from vibrant corners of the world are choosing Dayananda Sagar University (DSU) as their gateway to academic excellence. Here, you'll join a dynamic community that celebrates diversity and fosters innovation, all while pursuing your degree. Experience a transformative educational journey surrounded by like-minded individuals and engage in a rich cultural tapestry that enhances your learning and personal growth. Step into a world of opportunities at DSU and become part of our global family!

Semester Exchange at DSU

Students from all corners of the globe are choosing Dayananda Sagar University (DSU) for an exciting semester exchange experience! Here, you’ll have the chance to learn alongside international peers, sharing ideas and perspectives that enrich your academic journey. This program not only fosters cross-cultural connections but also enhances your global outlook, preparing you for a future in our interconnected world.

Global Partnerships at DSU

Dayananda Sagar University (DSU) opens the door to exciting collaborative opportunities for universities seeking to join hands with us. This partnership fosters academic excellence and facilitates the exchange of knowledge, resources, and expertise, benefiting both institutions. By collaborating with DSU, partner universities can enhance their global presence, strengthen their academic offerings, and contribute to a shared vision of advancing education on an international scale. Join us in shaping the future of higher education together!

Dayananda Sagar Institutions was founded in the 1960s, and Dayananda Sagar University (DSU) was established in 2014. DSU is renowned for its world-class interdisciplinary programs, including Medicine, Engineering, Business, Design, Computing, Law, and Science. It has also been accredited with a NAAC A+ rating.
